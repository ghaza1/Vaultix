Document Security

Protecting your sensitive documents with industry-leading encryption and security measures.

How We Secure Your Documents

End-to-End Encryption

All documents are encrypted using AES-256 encryption, ensuring that your sensitive information remains protected both in transit and at rest. Only authorized users with the proper credentials can access the decrypted contents.

Digital Signatures

Every document is digitally signed to verify its authenticity and ensure it hasn't been tampered with. Our system uses RSA cryptography to create unique signatures that can be verified at any time.

Access Controls

Granular permission settings allow you to control exactly who can view, edit, or sign your documents. Our role-based access control ensures that only authorized personnel can interact with sensitive information.

Audit Logging

Comprehensive audit logs track all document activities, including uploads, downloads, views, and signature events. This creates an immutable record of all interactions with your documents.

Security Best Practices

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication

    Add an extra layer of security to your account by enabling 2FA in your profile settings.

  • Regularly Review Access Permissions

    Periodically audit who has access to your documents and revoke unnecessary permissions.

  • Monitor Audit Logs

    Regularly check the audit logs for any suspicious activity related to your documents.

  • Use Strong Passwords

    Create unique, complex passwords for your account and change them periodically.
