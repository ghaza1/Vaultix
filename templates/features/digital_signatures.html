Digital Signatures

Secure, legally binding signatures that verify document authenticity and protect against tampering.

How Our Digital Signatures Work

Cryptographic Security

Our digital signatures use RSA public-key cryptography to create a unique, tamper-evident seal on your documents. Each signature is mathematically linked to both the document content and the signer's identity.

Verification Process

When a document is signed, our system creates a hash of the document content and encrypts it with the signer's private key. Anyone with access to the document can verify its authenticity using the signer's public key.

Signer Authentication

Before signing, users must authenticate their identity through our secure login process, which can include two-factor authentication for additional security.

Digital Certificates

Each signature includes a digital certificate that contains information about the signer, timestamp, and verification status, providing a complete audit trail for every signed document.

Benefits of Digital Signatures

Enhanced Security

Cryptographically secure signatures that cannot be forged or tampered with.

Increased Efficiency

Sign documents instantly from anywhere, eliminating the need for printing and scanning.

Legal Validity

Compliant with e-signature laws and regulations worldwide.

Audit Trail

Complete record of who signed what and when, with verification status.
