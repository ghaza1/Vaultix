Approval Workflow System

How It Works

Our approval workflow system provides a structured process for reviewing and approving both users and documents within your organization:

1

Submission

New users register or documents are uploaded to the system, automatically entering the approval queue with a "pending" status.

2

Review

Administrators review pending items in the approval queue, examining details and verifying information for compliance and security.

3

Decision

Administrators make an approval decision, either approving the item or rejecting it with a reason for the rejection.

4

Notification

The system automatically notifies relevant users about the approval decision and next steps.

5

Access Control

Based on the approval status, the system grants or restricts access to the approved items, maintaining security and compliance.

Key Features

User Approval

Control who can access your system by requiring administrator approval for new user registrations.

Document Approval

Ensure all documents meet your organization's standards before they become available in the system.

Audit Trail

Complete record of all approval activities, including who approved what and when.

Rejection Feedback

Provide detailed reasons for rejection to help users understand what needs to be corrected.

Approval Dashboard

Administrators can efficiently manage approvals through our dedicated dashboard.

The dashboard provides:

  • Queue of pending approvals
  • Detailed item information
  • Batch approval capabilities
  • Approval statistics
