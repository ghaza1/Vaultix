Frequently Asked Support Questions

Our standard support hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time. Premium support plans include extended hours and weekend coverage.

Our response times vary based on the priority of the issue:

  • High Priority: 1-2 business hours
  • Medium Priority: 4-8 business hours
  • Low Priority: 1-2 business days

Premium support plans have accelerated response times for all priority levels.

Phone support is available for Premium and Enterprise plan customers. If you're on one of these plans, you can find your dedicated support number in your account dashboard. Standard plan customers receive support via email and our support portal.

When you submit a support request, you'll receive a confirmation email with a unique ticket number. You can use this number to track the status of your request through our support portal. You'll also receive email updates whenever there's activity on your ticket.
